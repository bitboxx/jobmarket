Netherlands Job Market Visualizer Inspired by karpathy.ai/jobs 85276NED 85517NED

A Dutch remake of the US job treemap, built from official CBS open data. Each rectangle is a 4-digit occupational group, and each area is proportional to the number of people working in that occupation.

Employment now uses the latest quarter available, 2025 Q4. Color switches between Q4-to-Q4 employment growth, median hourly pay, women share, and higher-education share. Pay remains 2024 annual because that CBS wage table is less current.
